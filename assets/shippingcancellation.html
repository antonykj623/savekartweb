Shipping & Cancellation Policy

Thank you for visiting and shopping at https://www.mysavekart.com. This Shipping Policy describes the rules regarding the shipping of products ordered from our website and application. The following terms and conditions constitute our Shipping & Cancellation Policy.

1. Shipment Processing Time

  • All FMCG orders are processed and delivered within 2 to 3 business days, excluding Sundays and Public Holidays.
  • All non-FMCG products are processed and delivered within 10–15 days, depending on the delivery location.
  • Delivery time depends on the product, area pin code, and may vary due to external factors.
  • High order volumes may delay shipments; allow additional transit time.
  • We will contact you via email or phone if a significant delay occurs.

2. Shipping Rates

Shipping cost is displayed at the final order payment page on our website and app.

3. Shipment Confirmation & Order Tracking

Shipment confirmation will be sent via SMS to your registered mobile number. Order status can be checked via the “My Orders” page on our website or app.

4. Wrong & Incomplete Address

  • You must provide accurate and complete delivery details.
  • If incorrect shipping details are provided, it may lead to delivery issues. Reshipment is not eligible, but a refund will be issued within 15 days.
  • To receive the product again, a new order must be placed.

5. Cancellation & Refund

  • You can cancel the order before it is shipped.
  • Returns can be initiated within 7 days from the billing date for returnable products. Refunds will be processed within 7–10 days upon receiving items in re-saleable condition.
  • If we cannot deliver the order, it will be cancelled, and a full refund issued.
  • In cases of “delivered” status without actual delivery, notify us within 48 hours to be eligible for a refund.

6. Contact Us

  • Website: www.mysavekart.com
  • Email: mail@mysaving.in

7. Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this policy periodically. Review it before using the website.
  • Continued use of our services implies agreement to the updated policy.
