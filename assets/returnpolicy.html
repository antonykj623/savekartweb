Return and Exchange Policy

1. Eligibility for Returns or Exchanges

  • You may request a return or exchange within 7 days from the date of delivery.
  • To be eligible, products must be unused, unopened, and in their original packaging.
  • Perishable items, including but not limited to food and beverages, are exempt from returns for hygiene reasons, except in the case of defects or damage.

2. Non-eligibility for Return or Exchange

  • Damages due to misuse of the product.
  • Incidental damage due to the malfunctioning of the product.
  • With tampered or missing serial/UPC numbers.
  • In case of FMCG products or non-returnable products, if an order is placed for the wrong product code, color, or incorrect product or service.
  • Any product that is returned without all original packaging and accessories, including the box, manufacturer's packaging, if any, and all other items originally included with the product/s delivered.

3. Defective or Damaged Products

  • In the rare event that you receive a defective or damaged product, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of receiving the item.
  • Provide clear photos of the defective or damaged product, and we will arrange a replacement or refund.

4. Process for Returns or Exchanges

  • To initiate a return or exchange, please contact our customer service team at mail@mysaving.in
  • Clearly state your order number, the reason for the return or exchange, and any relevant details.
  • Our team will provide you with instructions on how to return the product or facilitate the exchange.

5. Return Shipping

  • We recommend using a trackable shipping method to ensure the safe return of the items.
